How it works

Recommends the next item to buy based on your champion, lane, and current items. Build data is fetched from Lolalytics (highest win rate build).

Key: Shows next item icon, name, and cost.
Dial: Rotate to browse the full 6-item build. Shows item cost, your gold, and a progress bar. "BUY NOW!" appears when you can afford the next item.

Champion and lane are auto-detected from the live game. Updates every 2 seconds during a game.
