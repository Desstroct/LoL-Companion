How it works

During Champion Select:
Analyzes your team and the enemy team composition.

Key: Shows quick balance summary (AD/AP heavy).

Dial:
• Rotate to switch between Ally/Enemy view
• Shows AD vs AP damage distribution
• Shows role breakdown (Tanks, Fighters, Mages, etc.)
• Highlights team strengths and weaknesses

Role codes: T=Tank, F=Fighter, A=Assassin, M=Mage, ADC=Marksman, S=Support
