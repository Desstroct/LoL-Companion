How it works

During Game:
Monitors enemies for important power spikes.

Level Spikes: Alerts when enemies hit level 6, 11, or 16 (ultimate upgrades).

Item Spikes: Alerts when enemies complete major items (Infinity Edge, Rabadon's, etc.).

Key: Shows spike count and latest event. Press to clear history.

Dial:
• Rotate to scroll through recent spike events
• Shows champion, spike type, and time since
• Visual alert on new high-priority spikes
