How it works

Shows the recommended jungle clear path based on your champion and map side. Automatically detects your champion during Champ Select (if assigned Jungle) or in-game (if you have Smite).

Key: Press to cycle between recommended path variants.
Dial: Rotate to scroll camp-by-camp through the path. Press to switch between path variants. Touch to refresh.

Camp positions are adjusted based on your side of the map:
• Blue Side: Blue buff = Bot, Red buff = Top
• Red Side: Blue buff = Top, Red buff = Bot

Supports 60+ junglers with tailored paths (power farmer, ganker, invader). Unknown champions will get default flexible paths.
